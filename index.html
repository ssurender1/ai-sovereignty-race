How to Play

  • You control one nation. The other 7 nations race via AI strategy.
  • Each turn = 1 year. The game spans 10 years (2025–2035).
  • You get 5 Action Points per turn to boost your 7 sovereignty levers.
  • Each +10 boost to a lever costs 1 AP.
  • A random global event each year affects all nations differently.
  • Sovereignty Score = average of all 7 levers, minus a penalty for your weakest lever.
  • First nation to 85+ sovereignty wins. After 10 years, highest score wins.
  • Player rating normalizes for country difficulty so harder nations earn more.
